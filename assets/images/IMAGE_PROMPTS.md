# Transyes AI Image Prompt List

## Usage

- Default style: photo-realistic premium corporate photography
- Aspect ratio:
  - Hero panel: `3:2`
  - Wide banner: `16:5`
  - Service icon: `1:1`
  - QR image: real QR only, not AI
- Avoid:
  - visible gibberish text
  - watermarks
  - fake logos
  - obvious AI skin texture or hand errors
  - 3D illustration look
  - overdone cinematic neon light

## Shared Direction

All visuals should look like real high-end corporate or editorial photography for a premium language-services brand. Prioritize believable people, lighting, materials, paper texture, glass reflection, fabric folds, and natural depth of field. Avoid illustrations, fake UI posters, floating objects, surreal composition, and stock-photo smiles.

## Must Not Be AI-Generated

- Client logos in the trust section
- WeChat QR codes


### `zh/home/hero-main.jpg`
Used by:
- `transyes/index.html`
- `transyes/zh/index.html`

Prompt:
Create a Chinese homepage hero image for a premium language-services brand. Show a real meeting scene where consultants and clients review multilingual documents in a modern conference room. The scene should communicate trust, cross-border communication, academic seriousness, and business reliability. Use a photo-realistic result with the look of premium corporate or editorial photography, not illustration, not 3D, not poster art, and not an obvious AI composite. Faces, hands, paper texture, fabric folds, glass reflections, screen glow, and depth of field must look physically believable. Avoid fake logos, readable brand names, watermarks, surreal lighting, floating objects, plastic skin, extra fingers, and stock-photo smiling. Keep the scene calm, credible, and high trust, with natural daylight or believable indoor mixed light, as if captured on a full-frame camera with a 50mm or 85mm lens. Use a restrained palette based on navy, warm ivory, graphite gray, pale wood, and subtle gold accents, but let those colors appear naturally through clothing, furniture, paper, and light rather than heavy color grading.
